This website was created to demonstrate some of the skills I have already learned thus far in the curriculum as well as show off one of my intrests...orchids!

For that reason, I spent a lot of time outside and loved watching everything flourish throughout the varying seasons, from short-lived spring tulips to hardy fall mums! I have a fond memory of going to the Missouri Botanical Garden to see their annual orchid show in march. The flowers had such a vast array of colors and patterns and shapes-there were so many different kinds!
